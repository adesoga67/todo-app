Our Story

Todo Wizard was born from a simple frustration: existing productivity tools were either too simple to be useful or too complex to be enjoyable. Our founders, both productivity enthusiasts and software engineers, spent countless hours switching between different apps, trying to find the perfect balance.

In 2023, we decided to build the tool we wished existed. Something that would be powerful enough for complex projects yet simple enough for daily task management. Something beautiful that you'd actually want to use.

Today, Todo Wizard helps millions of users worldwide stay organized, focused, and productive. But we're just getting started. Every feature we build is guided by one principle: make productivity feel effortless.
